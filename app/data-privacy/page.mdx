# Data & Privacy

- Matcha runs entirely on your machine.
- No data is collected, transmitted, or stored externally.
- No telemetry, no analytics, no phone-home.
- All power readings stay in your process memory and are discarded when the command exits.
- Matcha does not read, modify, or access your training data, model weights, or credentials.
